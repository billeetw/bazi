# Ten Gods UX — Archetype Layer (Canonical Copy)

Goal:
- Keep original Ten Gods terms (比肩/劫財/偏印/正印/食神/傷官/偏財/正財/七殺/正官)
- Add a human-readable archetype label + 3-part guidance:
  1) Core drive
  2) Risk when over-amplified
  3) Practical move (next action)

Tone:
- Ancient intelligence, modern clarity
- No fortune telling
- No hype

---

## 比肩 — 同行者 (Peer Drive)
EN: Peer Drive

Core:
- You strengthen through consistency, repetition, and shared standards.
Risk:
- Stagnation from staying "too equal" and avoiding differentiation.
Next move:
- Pick one domain to differentiate: skill, taste, or responsibility.

---

## 劫財 — 競合者 (Rival Drive)
EN: Rival Drive

Core:
- You grow through competition, pressure, and fast adaptation.
Risk:
- Impulsive decisions and resource leakage under comparison pressure.
Next move:
- Set one boundary: time, money, or attention — then defend it for 30 days.

---

## 偏印 — 轉化者 (Catalyst / Pattern Shifter)
EN: Catalyst Drive

Core:
- You learn by reframing, remixing, and turning chaos into insight.
Risk:
- Overthinking and detachment from real execution.
Next move:
- Convert one insight into one deliverable within 48 hours.

---

## 正印 — 支援者 (Support / Foundation)
EN: Support Drive

Core:
- You stabilize through knowledge, mentorship, and proper grounding.
Risk:
- Dependence on preparation; slow start.
Next move:
- Start imperfectly: 20 minutes of action before more research.

---

## 食神 — 建造者 (Builder / Sustainer)
EN: Builder Drive

Core:
- You win through quality, craft, and steady output.
Risk:
- Comfort traps: polishing instead of shipping.
Next move:
- Ship one "good enough" version; refine after feedback.

---

## 傷官 — 突破者 (Breaker / Challenger)
EN: Breaker Drive

Core:
- You create advantage by challenging norms and improving systems.
Risk:
- Friction with authority; cutting too hard, too fast.
Next move:
- Challenge one rule, but propose one replacement system.

---

## 偏財 — 機會者 (Opportunity / Deal Sense)
EN: Opportunity Drive

Core:
- You spot opportunities via networks, timing, and flexible strategy.
Risk:
- Chasing too many signals; unstable focus.
Next move:
- Choose one bet; define entry, exit, and risk cap.

---

## 正財 — 積累者 (Accumulator / Stability)
EN: Accumulator Drive

Core:
- You build power through reliability, compounding, and discipline.
Risk:
- Over-conservatism; missing windows.
Next move:
- Allocate a small "exploration budget" to keep growth alive.

---

## 七殺 — 壓力者 (Pressure / Edge)
EN: Pressure Drive

Core:
- You move when stakes are real; decisive under constraint.
Risk:
- Burnout and conflict escalation.
Next move:
- Define one non-negotiable recovery block each week.

---

## 正官 — 秩序者 (Order / Authority)
EN: Order Drive

Core:
- You operate best with structure, responsibility, and clear rules.
Risk:
- Rigidity; fear of making "wrong" moves.
Next move:
- Write a simple decision policy: what you will/won't do this season.
