什麼是人生說明書？

人生說明書是一套結合 八字、紫微斗數、五行與流年分析 的個人命盤解析系統，透過你的出生時間，整理出專屬的人生藍圖與角色設定。它不是單純的算命工具，而是一種幫助你理解自己、做出更清晰選擇的參考地圖。

八字以出生年、月、日、時為基礎，分析五行能量分佈，了解你的性格特質、優勢與潛在課題；紫微斗數則透過十二宮位，觀察事業、感情、財運與人生方向的發展結構。當八字與紫微交叉解讀，能更立體地呈現你的命盤樣貌。

流年與流月則提供時間維度的觀察，幫助你掌握 2026 流年運勢與關鍵節奏，看清哪些時期適合行動、哪些階段需要調整。五行平衡的狀態，也能提醒你如何補強能量，讓決策更穩定。

透過人生說明書，你可以重新認識自己的角色設定，理解天賦與限制，找到屬於你的主線任務。當方向清晰，人生就不再只是被動等待，而是主動參與的一場旅程。了解自己，是走向無悔人生的第一步。

What Is the Life Manual?

The Life Manual is a personalized life analysis system that integrates BaZi (Four Pillars of Destiny), Zi Wei Dou Shu, the Five Elements, and annual forecasts to create a structured view of your unique life blueprint. Based on your birth date and time, it generates a comprehensive birth chart and translates it into a clear character profile and life direction guide.

BaZi analyzes your year, month, day, and hour of birth to understand the balance of the Five Elements—Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water. This elemental structure reveals core personality traits, natural strengths, decision-making patterns, and potential life challenges. Rather than predicting fate, BaZi provides insight into your inherent energy design.

Zi Wei Dou Shu complements this system by mapping your life across twelve key life domains, including career, relationships, wealth, health, and long-term development. It offers a structural perspective on how different areas of your life interact and evolve over time. When BaZi and Zi Wei Dou Shu are interpreted together, they form a multidimensional view of your birth chart.

Annual and monthly forecasts add a time-based layer to this analysis. By examining your 2026 annual forecast and life cycles, you can better understand when to advance, when to consolidate, and when to adjust direction. This timing awareness helps you align your actions with favorable periods and navigate challenges with greater clarity.

The Five Elements framework also highlights energetic imbalances. Understanding which elements are strong or lacking allows you to make more grounded decisions in career, relationships, and personal growth.

The Life Manual is not about superstition or rigid destiny. It is a strategic self-understanding tool. By recognizing your character settings, talents, and life path patterns, you gain perspective on your main life themes and long-term trajectory. When you understand your design, life becomes less reactive and more intentional. The Life Manual helps you participate actively in your journey—so you can move forward with clarity, direction, and fewer regrets.

常見問題

為什麼紫微斗數比八字更立體？
紫微斗數以十二宮位呈現人生維度，八字則以四柱五行看能量結構。兩者交叉解讀，能更立體地呈現命盤樣貌。
李伯彥的人生說明書和傳統算命差在哪？
人生說明書不是預測吉凶，而是提供結構與節奏的參考地圖，幫助你做決策、找主場，而非被動等待命運。
五行比例高低代表什麼？
五行比例反映你的能量結構：木火偏旺者行動力強，金水偏旺者思考與收斂較強。了解比例有助補強弱項、發揮優勢。
太歲真的會影響運勢嗎？
太歲依地支關係（值、沖、刑、害、破）影響當年節奏。本服務依傳統規則推算，供你參考年度天時。
小限與流年差別是什麼？
小限依年齡走宮，流年依當年地支。小限看個人節奏，流年看大環境。兩者結合可更精準掌握時機。
